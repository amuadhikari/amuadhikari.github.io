Prefabricated
Housing

This is my first project as a graduate student at Virgina Tech. It is a sustainable prototype of an affordable single family house for the Orange county sustainability decathalon 2023. The design was developed on the basis of technological innovation, optimised performance, sensible use of materials and environmental concerns. The 1115 sqft. house is made of several modules. These prefabricated cartridges and panelised building parts could be manufactured in a factory under controlled conditions of temperature and humidity leading to higher efficiency in construction and greatly reducing environmental pollution. The modular components would be transported to the site on standard oversized trailers, thereby reducing onsite construction costs and time.

This is one of my first projects in VT.
