Parliament House Annexe

(Professional Project- Junior Architect)

3 story structure with two blocks spread over 39,495 square meters.

Parliament House annexe extension has rooms and offices of various parliamentary committees. The building has committee rooms, an auditorium, a banquet hall, and chambers for chairpersons of standing committees. It supports in the functioning of the Parliament. The well-furnished building has several large rooms where members of the Parliament can meet to discuss on important issues.

Located at Pandit Pant Marg, Sansad Marg Area, New Delhi, India.

Architect: Raja Aederi Consultants Pvt. Ltd., New Delhi.

Junior Architect roles and responsibilities:

• Produced interior design drawings and detail drawings.

• Prepared renderings and graphic material.

• Produced rendering material for client meetings.

• Assisted with site coordination.
